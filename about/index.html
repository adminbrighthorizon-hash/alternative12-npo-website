About Us

Locally led. Community trusted. Impact driven.

Alternative 12 partners with communities across Africa to uplift children — especially those living with disabilities and in underprivileged settings — through inclusive education, healthcare, rehabilitation, and family support services.

Our Approach

Building inclusive systems for the children who need them most.

Alternative 12 works through disability organizations, inclusive schools, rehabilitation centres, and local government partners. We do not create parallel systems — we strengthen existing institutions to serve children with disabilities and those from underprivileged backgrounds.

Our model integrates inclusive education, disability services, health and rehabilitation, and child protection into a coordinated approach that removes barriers and creates opportunity.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do.

Local Leadership

Programs are designed and delivered with disability organizations, inclusive educators, therapists, and community leaders already doing the work.

Dignity & Respect

We center the voices and agency of the communities we serve, working alongside families rather than around them.

Accountability

Every intervention ties to clear outcomes, regular field reporting, and shared learning with donors and local partners.

Inclusion

We connect inclusive education, disability services, health, and family resilience instead of treating them as separate problems.
