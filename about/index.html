Who We Are

COPAK GENERAL TRADING (CGT) is a leading Congolese provider of complete industrial solutions.

Since its inception, CGT has focused on delivering high-quality services from industrial equipment supply to installation and maintenance. Our expertise spans electrical, mechanical, instrumentation, electrowinning, conveyor accessories, pumps, valves, filters, reagents, chemicals, laboratory equipment, PPE, and critical equipment spares.

With a team of seasoned experts, we provide customized solutions tailored to each client's specific needs, ensuring quality outcomes and long-term value.
