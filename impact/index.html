From the Field

Stories of inclusion and change.

Northern Ghana

Back to school with the right support

After receiving adapted learning materials, a wheelchair, and dedicated mentoring, Kwame — a boy with cerebral palsy — returned to class and now reads to his younger siblings every evening.

Western Kenya

Early screening changes everything

Community volunteers identified hearing loss in 6-year-old Akinyi during a routine screening visit. With hearing aids and classroom support, she is now thriving in an inclusive school.

Kinshasa, DR Congo

A mother finds her village

Marie, raising twin daughters with visual impairments alone, joined a caregiver support group. Today she trains other parents and advocates for disability rights in her community.
